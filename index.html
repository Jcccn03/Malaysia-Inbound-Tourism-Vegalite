Malaysia Inbound Tourism

Where Tourists Come From?

(2018-2023)


How Main Source Markets Change Over Time?

Singapore consistently ranks first as the top source of visitors over the years, maintaining a strong lead in visitor numbers compared to other countries.

How Tourists Enter Malaysia?

Land crossings are the most common mode of entry for international tourists, with major checkpoints like Tambak Johor (BSI) and Gelang Patah (KSAB) handling the bulk of arrivals. Air travel via KLIA and KLIA 2 follows closely, while sea and rail modes contribute smaller numbers. Travel fell sharply in 2020–2021 due to restrictions, but arrivals across all modes rebounded in 2022–2023.

How Tourists Spend in Malaysia?

The expenditure breakdown shows that shopping consistently accounts for the largest share of tourist spending, followed by accommodation and transport.


COVID-19 pandemic in 2020–2021 caused a sharp decline in spending across all categories, but spending rebounded strongly in 2022–2023.

Distribution of Tourists to Parks in Malaysia

International tourists not only visit the top 3 parks but also show strong interest in marine destinations like Tun Sakaran and Terengganu Marine Park, while inland parks and wildlife centers attract eco-tourists seeking unique experiences.
